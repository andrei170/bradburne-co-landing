Our Story

Founded in Cupar in 1994. Rooted in St Andrews since 2000.

Bradburne & Co began as a rural firm of Chartered Surveyors based in Cupar. In 2000 the practice moved to its current office on South Street, in the heart of St Andrews, to serve both town and country clients under one roof.

More than three decades on, the firm remains independent and locally led, advising the same families and farms across Fife that the chain branches simply visit.

Common Questions

Answers before you pick up the phone

A few of the questions we are asked most often about buying, selling and letting through our St Andrews office.

Do you cover golf-front and East Neuk coastal property?
Yes. We regularly act on golf-side and coastal homes around St Andrews and along the East Neuk coast, including Crail, Anstruther, Pittenweem and Elie. These properties draw second-home and relocating buyers, so we present them with that audience in mind.
What areas do you cover?
We work across St Andrews, the East Neuk and North East Fife, including Cupar and the surrounding rural hinterland, all from our single office on South Street.
What is a RICS Chartered Surveyor and why does it matter?
A RICS Chartered Surveyor is a property professional regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors and held to its professional and ethical standards. For you it means valuations and survey advice are prepared by a qualified, regulated person rather than a general sales agent.
Do you handle lettings as well as sales?
Yes. Alongside sales we manage residential and student lettings as a Scottish Government Registered Letting Agent (LARN1810004), including the busy St Andrews student market.
How do I book a valuation?
You can call our St Andrews office on 01334 479 479, email info@bradburne.co.uk, or send us a message using the contact form on this page. We will arrange a time to visit and discuss your property.
Are you independent or part of a chain?
We are independent. Bradburne & Co has been a locally led, independent firm since 1994, advising the same families and farms across Fife rather than reporting to a regional head office.
Where is your office?
Our office is at 139 South Street, St Andrews, Fife KY16 9UN, in the heart of the town. We are open Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm, with viewings available seven days a week.
